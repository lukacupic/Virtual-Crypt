SANCTUARIUM ADIGNANI

HR | Što je “Sanctuarium Adignani”?
“Sanctuarium Adignani”, koliko je poznato, je među danas najvećim zbirkama relikvija koje dolaze iz vremena od gotovo 3000 godina s čitavog područja Rimskog i Bizantskog carstva. Skupljane su u Veneciji kroz tisuću godina. Kad su 1810. francuski vojnici stigli u Veneciju uništili su 120 od 200 crkvi. Tada je akademski slikar Gaetano Gresler skupljao i čuvao relikvije u svojoj palači Lezze. Godine 1818. preveo ih je jedrenjakom u Vodnjan gdje Francuza više nije bilo.

EN | What is “Sanctuarium Adignani”?
“Sanctuarium Adignani”, as far as it's known, is the largest collection of relics from the Roman and the Byzantine empires from a period of almost 3000 years. They were collected in Venice over a millennia. In 1810, French soldiers arrived in Venice and destroyed 120 out of 200 churches. Gaetano Gresler, a contemporary academic painter was a collector of these relics which he kept in his Lezze palace. In the year 1818, after the French soldiers left, he transported them to Vodnjan with his sailboat.
HR | Sveci su najvažnije ličnosti čovječanstva. Njihova moralna snaga i hrabrost zadivljuju sve generacije. Zato se njihove relikvije od 6. stoljeća ugrađuju u oltare i praktično se, kao uzori, nalaze u svim gradovima svijeta. Relikvije se svetaca nazivaju „moći“, jer po njima Bog kojemu su služili, čini čudesa. Već je šetnja Svetištem blagodat. Posjet ovog mjesta čovjeku vraća životnu snagu, što potvrđuju brojni posjetnici. Svojoj donacijom možete pomoći izgradnji jedinstvenog svetišta za dobrobit ljudi.

EN | Saints are the most important figures of humanity. Their moral strength and courage inspire all generations. That's why their relics have been used in altars since the 6th century in cities all over the world. Relics are called "powers" because according to them, the God they served was doing miraculous deeds. Walk through the sanctuary by itself is considered as a tremendous benefit. A visit to such a place replenishes strength, something which many visitors can attest to. With your donation, you can support the construction of a unique sanctuary for the wellbeing of the people.
